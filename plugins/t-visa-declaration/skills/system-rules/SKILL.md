## CRITICAL RULES (Case-Killing if Violated)

### 1. VAWA-vs-TRAFFICKING FILTER
Every trafficking paragraph must connect MEANS to ENDS (labor/servitude/financial exploitation). Abuse without End = VAWA = case killed.
- Substance/body control VAWA trap: forced drug use, alcohol, sleep deprivation MUST connect to trafficking Ends

### 2. MEANS-ENDS MANDATORY PAIRING
Every Means paragraph explicitly tied to specific End. Orphaned Means = VAWA.

### 3. COERCED COMPLIANCE
All compliance fear-driven, never voluntary. NEVER: "my goal was to keep the peace"

### 4. FIRST-PERSON SINGULAR
"I/me" only. NEVER "us/we/our" for victim group. HARD FAIL.

### 5. FIRING FEAR PROHIBITION
NEVER "afraid they would fire me" — implies voluntary employment. HARD FAIL.

### 6. ENFORCEMENT MECHANISM (ELEVATED)
Every forced action specifies HOW — with SPECIFIC consequences. Vague consequences = fail.

### 7. ENGLISH-ONLY
No Spanish anywhere. HARD FAIL.

### 8. ISOLATION INTEGRITY
Do NOT include details proving victim had outside support (third-party exit advice, family visits, dual-persona).

## ACTIVE VOICE AND LANGUAGE

- **Trafficker as grammatical subject** of action verbs
- **Journey/process narration** = high-frequency passive violation zone
- **Sexual violence**: "sexual slave/servitude" NEVER "sexually abused/assault"
- **Plain language**, no Spanglish idioms
- **Threats as direct quoted speech**

## STRUCTURAL RULES

### Introduction
- NO heading
- Flows directly under declaration title

### Background
- STRICTLY 2-3 paragraphs
- No US vulnerability paragraph (US-met trafficker)
- No intent to remain
- No forward references

### Trafficking Section
- First paragraph = process/setup (promises, not exploitation)
- Agreement framed around PROMISES not victim's needs
- Recruitment progression (2-4 paragraphs for gradual cases)
- Exploitation onset marker (when relationship predates exploitation)
- Labor paragraph granularity (separate focused paragraphs by labor category)
- Servitude contrast for ALL forced labor
- Surveillance as trafficking tool, not jealousy
- Wage exploitation formula (hours worked vs. hours paid)
- Insult content specificity (actual insults, not generic "insults")
- Threat transmission specificity (WHO, WHEN, WHAT exact words)
- Escape barriers explicitly addressed
- Scene-grounding (at least one specific trigger scene per major control method)
- Conversational authenticity (3-5 speech markers)
- Trafficking aftermath paragraph (if significant consequences exist)
- Child separation: parent wanted child but unable

### Physical Presence
- 1-2 paragraphs, 150-300 words. Trafficking-nexus only.
- Brief reference to trafficking, then focus on present consequences
- No re-narration of trafficking incidents

### Extreme Hardship
- 3 paragraphs max, 250-400 words. Must cite INA/CFR. Country conditions required.
- Forward-looking: no re-narration of trafficking, focus on consequences of removal

### Law Enforcement
- 1 paragraph. Altruistic framing.

### Conclusion
- 3-5 sentences. Plea not summary.

### Total Word Count
- 2,500-3,500 words
- FAIL if >4,000 words
